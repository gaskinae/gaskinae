Plus, in 2022, Dr. Lara Marks interviewed me on my pandemic experiences for her project documenting the history of COG-UK.

I love standardisation! So, I've been developing best practices for bioinformatics workflows at PHA4GE (Public Health Alliance for Genomic Epidemiology), an international pathogen genomics consortium. Publication coming soon.
